Preface

This book contains all possible bridge deals, one deal per page. The 53,644,737,765,488,792,839,237,440,000 pages stretch from one end of the universe to the other.

Due to its size, we cannot provide you with a full copy of the book, but ask our librarians for a few pages, by number, they can fetch you a page.

The first 10,400,600 pages in the Andrews and Pavlicek editions of the book have north holding all 13 spades and east all 13 hearts. That is a lot of deals, but still only an infinitesimal part of the book.

If you want more representative deals, you can select the scrambled version of either book. These are not randomized, but they will look more like normal deals for small page numbers.
